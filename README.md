**How to Use:**

2. Enter your desired keywords along with any demographic filters (e.g., gender, location) to refine your search.

### Facebook Account Warm-Up

Ensuring your Facebook account appears credible and engaging is critical, especially when initiating new interactions. The **Facebook Account Warm-Up** feature simulates genuine user behavior, allowing your account to gain trust and avoid restrictions. This includes scrolling through news feeds, interacting with posts, and avoiding excessive automated actions that could flag your account.

**How to Implement:**
1. Navigate to the **Facebook Account Warm-Up** section in MadHub.
2. Set parameters for interaction frequency, including the percentage of likes and comments to be executed.
3. Choose the duration for the warm-up phase and specify the type of content to engage with (e.g., videos, images).
4. Activate the feature to start gradually building your account’s credibility.
5. Regularly assess the account's activity levels and adjust settings accordingly to maintain a natural interaction flow.

**Effectiveness:** By employing this strategy, you can enhance your account's activity levels, strengthen trust with the Facebook algorithm, and create a sustainable environment for further marketing efforts.
